Hades-meets-Souls Combat

Wield both steel and sorcery: Start every new run by choosing from three unique weapon–spell combos, sometimes already infused with a modifier. As you progress, unlock more spells and adapt your style—go all-in on melee, lean into spellcasting, or weave the two together for devastating synergy.

Explore a Fragmented Fantasy World

Navigate through an ever-shifting map of fractured paths. Each node grants a distinct reward, and you can glimpse part of what lies ahead to plan your route and weigh tradeoffs. Along the way, uncover fragments of the world’s mystery and meet NPCs whose stories entwine with your own.

Build for the Run, Power for Eternity

Shape each of your runs with relics, talents, and spell–weapon combos, upgrading and infuse-modifying them as you go. Even when you fall, essence carries forward—fueling permanent upgrades that make future builds deadlier and let you chase the playstyles you love.

Key Features

  • Hades-meets-Souls Combat: Mix weighty melee strikes, impactful spells, and precise dodges against challenging enemies and bosses.
  • Weapon–Spell Choices: Start every run by picking from three unique weapon–spell combos, sometimes already infused with modifiers.
  • Build Crafting: Shape each run with relics, talents, spells, and infusions, then upgrade them mid-run for growing power.
  • Permanent Progression: Collect mythic essence during runs and spend it at the hub to permanently strengthen your soul.
  • Branching Paths: Navigate an ever-shifting node-based map where each path offers distinct rewards and tradeoffs.
  • Relics of the Gods: Seize divine relics left behind by vanished gods, each with combat-defining power.
  • Mystery and NPCs: Uncover fragments of the world’s collapse and meet characters whose stories entwine with your own.
