About

An upcoming roguelite soulslike set in a broken, ever-changing fantasy world where gods have fallen silent. Choose your class, tactically battle enemies and bosses, collect unique loot, unlock talents, and permanently enhance your character. Die, learn, and rise stronger.

Release target Q3 2025 - to be available on Steam for Windows.

Screenshots

Soulslike Combat

Engage in challenging combat against formidable enemies and bosses. Utilize a mix of melee attacks and spells, relying on well-timed dodges and parries to survive. Choose from different classes, each offering a unique weapon, spells, and talents that fit your playstyle.

Explore a Fragmented Fantasy World

Navigate through a broken, ever-changing realm where gods have fallen silent. Chart your own course through multiple routes filled with secrets and mysteries to uncover. Each path offers new challenges and discoveries in this dynamic world.

Permanent Progression and Customization

Collect different mythic essences to unlock permanent upgrades that enhance your character across all iterations. Hunt for powerful items and the perfect Eternalized Legacy to customize your abilities. Grow stronger with each attempt, as your progression persists beyond death.
