Privacy Policy

Controller: Shifting Reality Interactive, Finland Last updated: 28 Sep 2025

Who We Are

We are Shifting Reality Interactive (Jukka Tykkyläinen). Contact for privacy matters: shiftingrealityinteractive@gmail.com.

What Data We Collect and Why

  • Unity Analytics (Unity Technologies) — anonymous gameplay stats (e.g., level progression, deaths, challenge retries) to improve the game. No personally identifiable information is collected. See Unity Privacy Policy.
  • Steam (Valve) — the game uses Steam services (e.g., achievements, cloud saves). We do not access or process your Steam account data. See Steam Privacy Policy.
  • Feedback Survey (Google Forms) — optional. The form does not request personal data; free-text fields may include personal data if you choose to provide it. Processed by Google. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Website — static; no cookies, trackers, or data collection.
  • Discord — community server only; we don’t collect or process user data there.

Legal Basis

  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): anonymous analytics to develop and improve the game.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): any information you voluntarily provide in survey free-text fields.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data. Processing is limited to the third parties named above under their own privacy policies.

Your Rights (EU GDPR)

  • Access, rectification, erasure
  • Restriction and objection to processing
  • Data portability (where applicable)
  • Withdraw consent at any time (does not affect prior processing)
  • Lodge a complaint with your Data Protection Authority

To exercise rights, email shiftingrealityinteractive@gmail.com. We may need limited information to locate your survey submission, if any.

Retention

Unity retains anonymous analytics per its policy. Optional survey responses are kept only as long as needed for game improvement and then deleted.

International Transfers

Third-party providers (Unity, Google, Valve) may process data outside the EU/EEA. Where applicable, they use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (see their policies).

Children

The game is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect children’s personal data.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
